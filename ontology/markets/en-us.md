# English (United States) (en-us)

- **Native Name**: English
- **Direction**: ltr

## Brand Identity

- **Brand Name**: Echocraft
- **Tagline**: Where ideas become sound
- **Positioning**: Your complete music creation studio in the cloud.

## Market Snapshot

US-based music creators, indie artists, producers, content creators, and hobbyists seeking fast, affordable, professional-quality music production in the cloud.

## Voice (Locale Voice DNA)

Confident · Innovative · Creator-first · Tech-forward but accessible · Empowering, not intimidating · Modern and forward-looking.

## Tone & Style Rules

Clear and direct · Short sentences · Benefit-driven messaging · No hype or fluff · Human, not corporate · Focus on speed, quality, and simplicity.

## Culture & Pragmatics

Emphasize independence, ownership, and creative control · Align with the US creator economy mindset · Highlight speed, efficiency, and professional output · Assume high digital literacy · Celebrate ambition and self-expression.

## Grammar & Mechanics

American English spelling · Active voice · Contractions encouraged (you're, it's, don't) · Avoid unnecessary jargon · Sentence case for UI labels · Keep sentences concise.

## UX / Microcopy Rules

Strong action verbs: Create, Start, Export, Share, Upgrade · Buttons under 4 words · Encouraging and solution-oriented · Error messages: explain what happened + next step · No blame language · No exclamation marks in system messages.

## Locale Conventions

Currency: USD ($) · Date: MM/DD/YYYY · Time: 12-hour AM/PM · Imperial units · Commas in thousands (1,000).

## Terminology Rules

"music creation" not "music generation" (unless AI-specific output)
"project" = user workspace · "track" = audio layer · "studio" = platform environment
AI features = "AI-powered", not "fully automated"
Avoid: "revolutionary", "disruptive", "game-changing"

## Golden Examples

Hero: Your complete music creation studio in the cloud.
CTA: Start creating free
Feature: Generate melodies with AI, refine them with pro tools, and export studio-ready tracks in minutes.

## Output Constraints

Headlines ≤ 12 words · Paragraphs ≤ 3 sentences · No emojis in core messaging · No slang (unless targeting Gen Z) · No overpromised outcomes · All claims must be realistic and product-aligned.
